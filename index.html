WOMBATS 2027

Workshop On Modular Behaviour Across Tensor Structures

University of Sydney · 29 November – 3 December 2027

About

WOMBATS 2027 will bring together researchers working in representation theory, monoidal and tensor categories, and related structures in positive characteristic.

The main themes include:

  • Exotic monoidal and tensor categories
  • Representation theory of reductive algebraic groups in positive characteristic
  • p-Kazhdan–Lusztig theory

The workshop is intended to be broad, with room for adjacent directions in modern representation theory.
